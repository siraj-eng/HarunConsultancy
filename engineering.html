Course Overview

Engineering and Technical courses represent the backbone of modern infrastructure and technological advancement. These fields focus on applying scientific principles, mathematics, and hands-on skills to design, build, and maintain everything from buildings and machines to complex systems and advanced technology.

As Kenya continues its development journey with projects like affordable housing, road networks, and industrial growth, the demand for skilled engineers and technicians has never been higher. This field is perfect for problem-solvers, innovators, and those who want to create tangible solutions that impact society.

Main Engineering Branches

Civil Engineering

Design and construct infrastructure including roads, bridges, buildings, and water systems that form the backbone of modern society.

  • Civil Engineer
  • Structural Engineer
  • Site Supervisor
  • Quantity Surveyor

Electrical Engineering

Design, develop, and maintain electrical systems, power generation, distribution networks, and electronic devices.

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power Systems Engineer
  • Control Engineer
  • Electronics Technician

Mechanical Engineering

Design, analyze, and maintain mechanical systems, machinery, manufacturing processes, and thermal systems.

  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Plant Engineer
  • Maintenance Manager
  • HVAC Engineer

Mechatronics Engineering

Combine mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering to create intelligent systems, robotics, and automated solutions.

  • Automation Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
  • Control Systems Engineer
  • Instrumentation Engineer

Chemical Engineering

Design and optimize chemical processes for manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, energy production, and materials development.

  • Process Engineer
  • Chemical Engineer
  • Quality Control Engineer
  • Petroleum Engineer

Computer Engineering

Design and develop computer hardware and software systems, embedded systems, and network infrastructure.

  • Computer Hardware Engineer
  • Network Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Systems Architect

Career Opportunities

Engineers and technicians are essential across all sectors of the economy:

Civil Engineer

Design and supervise construction of infrastructure projects including roads, bridges, and buildings.

Salary: Ksh 60,000 - 500,000+
Electrical Engineer

Design, implement, and maintain electrical systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Salary: Ksh 55,000 - 450,000+
Mechanical Engineer

Design, analyze, and maintain mechanical systems and machinery for manufacturing and production.

Salary: Ksh 58,000 - 480,000+
Automation Engineer

Design and implement automated systems for manufacturing, processing, and industrial applications.

Salary: Ksh 70,000 - 600,000+
Technical Officer

Install, maintain, and repair equipment and systems across various industries and sectors.

Salary: Ksh 35,000 - 200,000+
Site Supervisor

Oversee construction projects, manage teams, and ensure quality and safety standards are met.

Salary: Ksh 45,000 - 350,000+

Industry Sectors: Construction, Manufacturing, Energy, Transportation, Telecommunications, Mining, Government, Consulting, and International Development.

Is Engineering Right for You?

This field is ideal if you:

  • Enjoy solving practical problems
  • Have strong mathematics and science skills
  • Are interested in how things work and are built
  • Enjoy hands-on work and creating tangible solutions
  • Have good spatial awareness and attention to detail
  • Want a career with high demand and good earning potential
  • Are patient and persistent in solving complex challenges

The Engineer's Mindset: Engineering requires logical thinking, creativity, and the ability to see the big picture while managing intricate details. It's a field that rewards innovation, precision, and the satisfaction of seeing your designs become reality.
