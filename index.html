About Harun Consultancy

Your trusted partner in career guidance and academic success

Transforming Futures Since 2015

At Harun Consultancy, we specialize in helping KCSE graduates navigate the complex world of career choices with confidence and clarity. Our approach combines data-driven insights with personalized guidance to ensure every student finds their perfect career match.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan students with the knowledge, tools, and confidence needed to make informed career decisions that align with their academic strengths and personal aspirations.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading career consultancy, recognized for transforming student potential into professional success through innovative guidance solutions.
