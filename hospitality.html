Course Overview

Hospitality and Tourism is the dynamic industry dedicated to creating exceptional experiences for guests, travelers, and customers. This field combines business management with service excellence, cultural understanding, and operational expertise to deliver memorable experiences.

As one of the world's largest and fastest-growing industries, hospitality and tourism offers diverse career paths that combine professional growth with exciting travel opportunities. Kenya's position as a top tourist destination makes this field particularly promising for students seeking dynamic, people-oriented careers.

Main Specializations

Hotel Management

Comprehensive training in hotel operations, front office management, housekeeping, food & beverage, and hotel marketing and sales.

  • Hotel Manager
  • Front Office Manager
  • Housekeeping Supervisor
  • Revenue Manager
Focus: Luxury hotels, resorts, boutique hotels

Culinary Arts

Professional training in food preparation, kitchen management, menu planning, nutrition, and restaurant operations.

  • Executive Chef
  • Sous Chef
  • Pastry Chef
  • Food & Beverage Manager
Focus: Fine dining, restaurants, catering

Tourism Management

Study of tourism systems, destination management, tour operations, travel agency management, and sustainable tourism.

  • Tourism Manager
  • Travel Consultant
  • Tour Operator
  • Destination Specialist
Focus: Travel agencies, tour companies, DMOs

Events Management

Planning, organizing, and executing events including conferences, weddings, corporate functions, and exhibitions.

  • Event Planner
  • Conference Manager
  • Wedding Coordinator
  • Exhibition Manager
Focus: MICE industry, social events

Food & Beverage

Specialized training in restaurant operations, bar management, beverage service, and hospitality business management.

  • Restaurant Manager
  • Bar Manager
  • Sommelier
  • Banquet Manager
Focus: Restaurants, bars, catering

Resort & Leisure

Management of resorts, spas, wellness centers, golf courses, and recreational facilities.

  • Resort Manager
  • Spa Director
  • Leisure Manager
  • Activities Coordinator
Focus: Resorts, cruise ships, wellness

Salary: Ksh 70,000 - 300,000+

Create menus, manage kitchen operations, control food costs, and maintain culinary standards.

Salary: Ksh 90,000 - 600,000+

Lead tourist groups, provide cultural and historical information, and ensure memorable experiences.

Salary: Ksh 40,000 - 200,000+

Plan and execute events from conception to completion, managing vendors, budgets, and client expectations.

Oversee entertainment, activities, and guest services on cruise ships sailing worldwide.

Is Hospitality Right for You?

This exciting industry calls for individuals who:

  • Enjoy working with people from diverse backgrounds
  • Thrive in fast-paced, dynamic environments
  • Have excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Are flexible with working hours (including weekends/holidays)
  • Possess cultural sensitivity and awareness
  • Have problem-solving abilities and remain calm under pressure
  • Are passionate about travel, food, and experiences
  • Want opportunities to work locally and internationally

The Hospitality Professional: This field offers more than just a job—it offers a lifestyle. You'll meet people from around the world, travel to exciting destinations, and build a career that's as dynamic as it is rewarding. While it requires hard work and dedication, the personal and professional growth opportunities are unparalleled.
